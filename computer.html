The Ramayana was written by Tulsidas known as Ramcharitmanas,is a sixteen century epic. The story resolves around Rama, who was the seventh avtar of Hindu god vishnu. He is the central figure of the hindu epic Ramayana, which is the principal narration of the events connected to his incranation on earth, His ideals and his greatness. He was the eldest son of King Dashraths- the ruler of Koshala Kindom. Rama's life and journey is one of adherence to dharma dispit harsh test dispite harsh tests and obstacles and many pains of the life and time. This adherence made him earn the name of Marayada Purushottam which literally means The Perfect Man or Lord of Self-control of Lord of virtue. for the sake of his father's honour, Rama abandons his claim to ayodhya's throne to serve an exile for fourteen years in the forest. His wife Sita and his brother lakshmana decided to join him, and all three spend the fourteen years in exile together. While in exile, sita is kidnapped by Ravana, the king of Lanka. After a long and aruduous search. Rama fights a colossal war against Ravana's armies. In a war of powerfull and magical beings, greatly distructive weapon and battles, Rama slays Ravana in battle and liberates his wife. Having completed his exile, Rama returns to be crowned king in Ayodhya and eventually becomes the emperor. The whole story is diveded into seven parts known as 'Kands'.
